Midnight Season 1 Raid Boss Cheat Sheets - Voidspire, Dreamrift, March on Quel'Danas

By Paryah Updated: 2026/03/16
Live

Follow:
Midnight Season 1 includes three raids: Voidspire, The Dreamrift, and March on Quel'Danas. Defeating each boss is made easier with organized and clear cheat sheets, detailing the fight style, key assignments, mechanics, and more, all in one place.

Each boss section includes a cheat sheet for that boss fight, as well as a link to our in-depth guide for the boss. Our in-depth guides include more information for each particular boss, including boss abilities and loot.

Imperator Averzian Cheat Sheet

Imperator Averzian wages a relentless campaign, seeking not conquest, but annihilation. His entire will is bent to a singular ambition, to rend the veil between worlds and carve a breach into the Void itself. Through this wound, his infinite host shall pour forth, drowning Azeroth in darkness.

Imperator Averzian Guide

Vorasius Cheat Sheet

A colossal predator born in the wastes of the Voidstorm, Vorasius grew to an incredible size fueled by an endless hunger. Summoned to the battle raging at the base of the Voidspire by Voidlight Everdawn, this colossal monster now feasts on both sides.

Vorasius Guide

Fallen-King Salhadaar Cheat Sheet

After turning on Xal'atath, the fallen king Salhadaar has become a captive of the very Shadowguard he once commanded. Tortured, delirious, and infused with the Void, he awaits his death--or a chance to win his people's freedom from Xal'atath's grasp.

Fallen-King Salhadaar Guide

Vaelgor & Ezzorak Cheat Sheet

Born as clutchmates in the red dragonflight, Vaelgor and Ezzorak were once the guardians of all living things. Now, twisted by the dark will of Xal'atath, all that remains of their former selves is their unyielding bond to one another.

Vaelgor & Ezzorak Guide

Lightblinded Vanguard Cheat Sheet

Once paragons of the Light, Senn, Bellamy, and Lightblood have surrendered to their own blinding zealotry--their faith perverted into a ruthless fanaticism that now compels them to smite all who oppose them.

Lightblinded Vanguard Guide

Crown of the Cosmos Cheat Sheet

High atop the Voidspire, Xal'atath marshalls her dark army from across the Voidstorm. Aided by Turalyon, Arator, and Alleria, the mortals of Azeroth must confront her before she unleashes the might of the Voidspire against her enemies.

Crown of the Cosmos Guide
